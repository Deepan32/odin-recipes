Rasam

Description:

The Rasam is a famous Indian dish. Its a spicy and full flavoured dish which is mostly eaten for lunch along with curd, onion which is called Raita and a brinjal gravy. The History of Rasam dates back to mogals. But relax we are not going there.. Chicken or mutton is added as a main ingredient. Some people go for vegetable Biriyani also but it is not technically a right word meaning that Biriyani is only a non-veg dish

The rice in Rasam soru should be Basmathi or a high quality grain which is also a main ingedient. Apart from non-veg pieces and rice, the other main ingredient is ghee and coconut oil. There are many types of Biriyanis available in India.
