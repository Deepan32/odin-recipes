Biriyani

Description:

The Biriyani is a famous Indian dish. Its a spicy and full flavoured dish which is mostly eaten for lunch along with curd, onion which is called Raita and a brinjal gravy. The History of Biriyani dates back to mogals. But relax we are not going there.. Chicken or mutton is added as a main ingredient. Some people go for vegetable Biriyani also but it is not technically a right word meaning that Biriyani is only a non-veg dish

The rice in Biriyaani should be Basmathi or a high quality grain which is also a main ingedient. Apart from non-veg pieces and rice, the other main ingredient is ghee and coconut oil. There are many types of Biriyanis available in India.

Ingredients

  • Chicken or Mutton
  • Ghee
  • Rice
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Clean the Chicken
  2. Soak the rice
  3. eat it
